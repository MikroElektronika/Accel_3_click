Calibrate X

Calibrates X sensitivity. The positive value is acheived by facing the click to the right , and the negative value is found by facing the click to your left.
Parameters
[in]pos- Value when click is turned to your right.
[in]neg- Value when click is turned to your left.

Calibrate Y

Calibrates Y sensitivity.The positive value is acheived by turning the click to face in front of you, and the negative value is found by turning the click towards you.
Parameters
[in]pos- Value when click is turned away from you.
[in]neg- Value when click is turned towards you.

int16_t  neg 
)

Calibrate Z

Calibrates Z sensitivity. This is done by turning the click towards the sky and recording the positive value and then turning the click towards the center of the earth and recording that value and sending those to Calibrate Z.
Parameters
[in]pos- Value when click is turned towards the sky.
[in]neg- Value when click is turned towards center of earth.
